What the platform does

It turns AI adoption from individual experimentation into a managed baseline.

Workplace AI Academy provides practical learning, structured pathways and progress visibility so organisations can set a common standard for safe and effective AI use.

  • Teach employees how to use AI safely in everyday work.
  • Build applied capability for teams using AI in practical workflows.
  • Help managers govern AI use, review outputs and support teams.
  • Give organisations evidence of completion, capability and adoption coverage.
Before the product

Read the problem first.

Workplace AI Academy is designed for the operating model issue described in the Workplace AI overview.

Course architecture

Three course areas, one consistent adoption baseline.

The Academy is structured around the practical capabilities organisations need first: safe use, applied use and management of AI in work.

Core baseline01

Workplace AI: Safe & Effective Use

For employees who need clear standards on appropriate use, prompting, data protection, output checking and accountability.

Practical application02

Applied AI

For teams ready to use AI in defined workflows, with attention to quality, evidence, handover and repeatable practice.

Manager pathway03

Managing AI in Work

For managers who need to guide teams, review AI-supported work, spot risk and keep human accountability clear.

How it works

Simple for learners. Useful for operators.

The Academy is built to create a consistent standard across the organisation, then give leaders enough visibility to manage adoption properly.

Assign

Set the right pathway.

Organisations assign baseline, applied or manager learning depending on role, risk and adoption maturity.

Learn

Complete practical modules.

Learners work through concise lessons focused on safe use, better prompts, human review, data handling and accountable decisions.

Evidence

Make progress visible.

Completion and pathway data help leaders understand coverage, gaps and where additional support is needed.

Improve

Use the evidence to govern adoption.

Admin visibility gives organisations a practical basis for coaching, reinforcement and governance conversations.

Learner experience

Clear lessons for the way people actually work.

The learner experience is designed to be direct, role-aware and practical. It helps people understand what good AI use looks like before tools become embedded in daily decisions.

01

Practical standards.

Clear guidance on when to use AI, when not to use it, and how to keep judgement in the loop.

02

Data and confidentiality.

Plain rules for handling sensitive information, customer context, personal data and internal material.

03

Output review.

Learners practise checking accuracy, assumptions, bias, tone, sources and suitability before using AI-supported work.

Deployment options

Designed for baseline rollout, targeted pathways or managed adoption programmes.

Organisations can use Workplace AI Academy as a broad foundation, a manager readiness programme, or a targeted pathway for teams already applying AI in operational work.

  • Organisation-wide baseline for safe and effective use.
  • Role-based pathways for managers, operators and AI-active teams.
  • Cohort rollout for specific functions or risk areas.
  • Evidence-led support for policy adoption and governance rhythm.
