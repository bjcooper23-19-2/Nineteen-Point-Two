Buyer problem

Revenue plans often fail before the pipeline does.

The issue isn't always effort. A target can look plausible while the model underneath is already under strain: deal volume, win rate, sales cycle, capacity, attainment and forecast reliability all carry hidden assumptions.

What it examines

The Revenue Stress Test checks the mechanics behind the plan.

Commercial assumptions

Target, average deal value, win rate, sales cycle and the activity required to carry the number.

Capacity and confidence

Whether the team and current operating rhythm can support the plan without hiding strain in the forecast.

First exposed point

Which assumption is most likely to break first if leadership adds pressure without changing the system.
