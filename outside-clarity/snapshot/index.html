Information handling

Outside Clarity is designed to work from concise, non-sensitive decision context.

Please do not submit confidential business information, trade secrets, customer data, employee data, credentials, contracts, financial records, legal documents or regulated information through this form.

Submissions may be reviewed using AI-supported tools and human quality control to produce the assessment.

If your plan requires sensitive material to assess properly, contact Nineteen Point Two first so the right handling approach can be agreed.
