This Cookie Notice explains how Nineteen Point Two uses cookies and similar technologies on its website and WAIA platform.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small files placed on your device when you visit a website or use an online service. Similar technologies may include local storage, pixels, tags, scripts and device identifiers.

These technologies can help a site work properly, remember preferences, support security, measure usage or support marketing activity.

3. Strictly necessary cookies

Strictly necessary cookies are used to provide services you have requested, such as secure login, authentication, account access, platform security and remembering cookie preferences.

These cookies cannot usually be switched off because the service would not work properly without them.

4. Analytics cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how people use the website or platform. For example, they may help us understand which pages are viewed, which content is useful, and where errors occur.

We will only use non-essential analytics cookies where required consent has been obtained.

Current analytics tools: Google Search Console only. No Google Analytics, Plausible, LinkedIn Insight Tag, Meta Pixel or advertising pixel is currently known to be active.

5. Marketing cookies

Marketing cookies may be used to understand campaign performance or support relevant advertising.

Current marketing cookies or pixels: We do not currently use marketing cookies or advertising pixels as far as currently known.

6. Managing cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through our cookie banner or settings tool where available.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect how the website or WAIA platform works.
